# New Features Guide (v1.1.0)

## üè¢ Multi-Organization Support

### What's New
You can now manage multiple inventories for different purposes - whether you're running a charity, food bank, personal pantry, or any other type of inventory.

### How to Use

#### Creating a New Organization
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Organizations**
2. Tap **"+ New Organization"**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Name**: Your organization name
   - **Type**: Choose from Charity, Food Bank, Pantry, Personal, or Other
   - **Description**: Optional description
4. Tap **Save**

#### Switching Between Organizations
1. On the **Dashboard**, tap the organization name at the top
2. Select the organization you want to switch to
3. All inventory data will update to show that organization's items

#### Managing Members
Organization owners can invite members:
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Organizations** ‚Üí Select your org
2. Tap **"Manage Members"**
3. Add members by email and assign roles:
   - **Owner**: Full access, can delete organization
   - **Admin**: Can manage items and members
   - **Member**: Can view and perform transactions

## üè™ Food Bank Marketplace

### What's New
Connect with other organizations to share or exchange surplus items! The Marketplace lets you:
- Post items you have available to share
- Request items you need
- Propose item exchanges with other organizations

### How to Use

#### Accessing the Marketplace
1. From the **Dashboard**, tap the green **"Food Bank Marketplace"** card
2. Or navigate directly to `/marketplace`

#### Browsing Listings
- Use the **search bar** to find specific items or organizations
- Use the **filter dropdown** to show:
  - **All Types**: Everything
  - **Sharing**: Items available for free
  - **Exchange**: Items available for trade
  - **Requests**: Items organizations are looking for

#### Posting a Listing

1. Tap **"+ Post Listing"** button
2. Select the **listing type**:
   - **Share**: You're giving items away
   - **Exchange**: You want to trade for something
   - **Request**: You're looking for items
3. Choose the **item** from your inventory
4. Enter the **quantity available**
5. Add an optional **description**
6. Set an **expiration date** (optional)
7. Tap **Post**

#### Responding to Listings

1. Find a listing you're interested in
2. Tap **"Contact"** to send a request
3. Enter the **quantity** you need
4. Add a **message** to the organization
5. Submit your request

#### Managing Requests

For listings you've posted:
1. Go to **Marketplace** ‚Üí **My Requests**
2. View incoming requests
3. **Approve** or **Reject** each request
4. Once approved, coordinate pickup/delivery

**Q: Can I use multiple organizations?**  
A: Yes! Create and manage as many as you need.

**Q: Who can see my listings?**
